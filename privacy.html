The Edge Bio Privacy Policy


At The Edge Bio, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we manage and safeguard your data. We adhere to global privacy standards including UK GDPR, PECA (Pakistan), HIPAA (USA), and other healthcare-compliant frameworks to ensure the safe and ethical use of AI in medical services.

1. Our Core Principle: Minimal Data, Maximum Privacy

● We do not collect or retain personal, medical, or sensitive data unless explicitly submitted through secure channels.

● We do not gather biometric, genetic, financial, or identity-related data.

● We do not use tracking cookies, third-party advertisements, or unauthorized analytics scripts.

2. No Biometric or Sensitive Data Collection

● Our AI systems are designed to support diagnostics, automation, and clinical workflows — not to process sensitive personal identifiers unless explicitly authorized and required for clinical use.

● We use:

○ Synthetic or anonymized datasets for training

○ Datasets approved with documented consent from medical institutions or research collaborators

3. Ethical AI Development & Testing

For diagnostics training, medical automation modules, and decision-support tools:

○ We use simulated data during internal development phases

○ Real clinical data is used only when appropriate permissions and consents are obtained

○ We do not engage in data scraping or unauthorized content harvesting from any public or private sources

4. Respect for Public & Clinical Data

● We do not collect content from social media, public forums, or patient databases without consent and contractual approval.

● Clients are fully responsible for ensuring all data provided — for testing, configuration, or AI training — is lawful and compliant with healthcare privacy standards.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

● We do not sell, rent, or transfer any personal or institutional data to third parties.

● Any data processed via third-party integrations (such as EHR systems or hospital platforms) remains under the control of the client or medical provider.

6. Contact Forms & Communication

● If you contact us via form or email, your information is used solely to respond to that request.

● We do not retain or use submitted details for marketing unless you opt in explicitly.

7. Global Privacy Law Compliance

We comply with applicable healthcare and data privacy regulations, including:

● UK GDPR & Data Protection Act 2018

● Pakistan PECA (Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act)

● U.S. HIPAA for clinical data privacy (when applicable)

● Industry best practices such as “Privacy by Design” and “Privacy by Default”

8. Platform Security & Data Integrity

● We implement strong encryption, access control, and secure communication protocols.

● Operational logs and temporary data are auto-deleted after use.

● By default, we do not store identifiable patient data on our systems unless contractually required.

9. Your Rights

If you voluntarily provide data and wish to:

● Review, correct, or delete it

● Understand our data policies more deeply

Please contact us at: support@theedgebio.com

10. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated as our services evolve. We encourage all users and partners to check for changes periodically. The "Last updated" date will reflect any revision.

✅ Summary:

At The Edge Bio, your privacy is respected, secured, and never compromised. We collect as little data as possible, and use only what’s necessary to provide cutting-edge AI solutions for the healthcare sector.
